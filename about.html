About Me

Ethan Cole

I am a Toronto strength and physique coach with 15+ years of coaching experience and a kinesiology-based training approach.

I work with beginners, returning lifters, and busy adults who want visible muscle and better strength without random workouts or reckless loading. My coaching blends practical gym experience with movement assessment, exercise physiology, and clear progression.

My goal is simple: help you train hard enough to change, smart enough to recover, and consistently enough to keep the result.

My coaching approach

Assess first

Movement quality, injury history, training age, recovery, and schedule shape the plan before volume or intensity goes up.

Progress with intent

Exercises, sets, reps, tempo, and load are chosen for a reason, then adjusted based on performance and recovery.

Stay inside the right scope

I am not a physician or physiotherapist. Medical diagnosis, treatment, or clearance belongs with licensed healthcare professionals.
