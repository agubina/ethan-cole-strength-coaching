Downtown · Entertainment District

1:1 coaching at Fit Squad Training Gym.

My downtown base on Peter St. Strength and physique coaching built for working professionals — efficient sessions you can fit around a busy office day in the core.

What training here looks like

Fit Squad is a big, modern 11,000 sq ft facility in the core — my pick for downtown professionals who train before work, on a lunch break, or right after the office.

With premium machines from Atlantis, Prime, Rogue, and Nautilus alongside a full free-weight floor, turf, and cardio, it is built for physique and hypertrophy work — we can isolate a muscle on a quality machine, then load the big lifts with free weights. A clean complement to the barbell-first room at Fortis.

  • 11,000 sq ft of training space
  • Upper & lower body machines
  • Full free-weight floor
  • Turf & functional area
  • Cardio & conditioning
  • Atlantis · Prime · Rogue · Nautilus
Best for physique & recomposition on a downtown schedule: premium machines for precise muscle work, free weights for the heavy lifts, and a coach on every set — walk in from the office, train, and get back to your day.
